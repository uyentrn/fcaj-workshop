# Key Takeaways: “First Cloud AI Journey & Tech Career Meetup”

### Event Purpose

- Share the personal growth journey from student to AWS Partner and Community Builder.
- Demystify the mindset, real-world work culture, and standard recruitment processes at Multinational Corporations (MNCs).
- Clarify the actual roles of DevOps/Cloud Engineers, analyzing the IT labor market landscape and salary trends.
- Provide guidance on holistic career development: Career paths, Soft skills, Systems thinking, and Community responsibility.

### Key Highlights

#### Roadmap from Student to AWS Partner & Community Builder
- **8-Stage Growth Journey**: From student curiosity (`Student Curiosity`), joining `First Cloud Journey`, attending `Workshops & Community`, engaging in `Hands-on Labs`, applying to `School Projects`, building a `Portfolio`, becoming an `AWS Partner`, to giving back (`Share Back`).
- **Community Opportunities & Benefits**: Privileges of joining the AWS Student Builder Group and Community Builders program (receiving AWS Credits, Exam Vouchers, Swag points, and networking opportunities with AWS Heroes & Advocates).

#### Real-World Data Analytics Engineering & MNC Culture
- **Real-world Work by Domain**:
  - *B2B E-commerce/Logistics Sector (Kamereo)*: Developing operational performance reports (GMV, AOV, Fill Rate, Backup Costs) and optimizing Fulfillment & Last-Mile costs.
  - *FMCG/Manufacturing Sector (Colgate-Palmolive)*: Processing IoT equipment data, optimizing manufacturing costs, and driving operational digital transformation.
- **Corporate Culture at MNCs**:
  - *No-Blame Post-Mortem (MNC Tech)*: Focusing on finding root causes to improve systems rather than assigning individual blame.
  - *Caring & Inclusive (MNC FMCG)*: Respecting diversity and placing people at the center of development.
- **5-Level Personal Growth Framework**: `Follower` (Executer) → `Learner` (Active Learner) → `Problem Solver` (Practical Problem Solver) → `System Thinker` (Systems Thinker) → `Super Star` (Leader & Strategy Architect).
- **The "Right Work" Philosophy**: Balancing Being Human (*Fulfillment*), Being a Professional (*Purpose & Meaningful*), and Being a Citizen (*Community & Legacy* – bearing the nation's digital backbone).

#### DevOps Engineering Career Realities & The IT Market
- **Hiring Trends & Salary Analysis (2025–2026)**: AI/ML, Data Engineering, Cloud Engineering, Security, and DevOps continue to lead in hiring demand and compensation packages.
- **The Reality of DevOps Work**: Far beyond writing pipelines or deploying code, DevOps Engineers are responsible for incident handling, troubleshooting, cloud cost optimization, and ensuring system-wide stability and security.
- **Foundational Learning Roadmap**: Emphasizing mastery of Linux, Networking, Git, Python/Golang, and Docker/Containers before rushing into complex tooling.
- **Impact of AI**: Leveraging AI (ChatGPT, Claude) to boost productivity without "switching off your brain"; the core lies in understanding fundamental concepts (*Fundamentals stay*).

### Key Learnings

#### Design & Systems Thinking
- **System Thinker Mindset**: Viewing problems through the big picture, evaluating interdepartmental connections, predicting operational risks and financial impacts rather than just applying "quick fixes."
- **Fundamentals First**: Tools change constantly, but foundational knowledge (Linux, Networking, Core CS) remains the unchanging core.
- **Business-Driven Data**: When analyzing or creating data reports, always investigate the root causes behind metric fluctuations (e.g., GMV changes) to identify actionable business improvements.

#### Soft Skills & Professional Competencies
- **Storytelling with Data**: The ability to translate dry numbers on dashboards into meaningful business narratives that empower leadership and teams to make decisions.
- **Communication & Proactive Inquiries**: Communication is an integral part of engineering work (*Communication is part of the job*); always ask "Why" before "How."
- **STAR Method in Interviews**: Preparing for standard MNC interview processes (English Screening → Aptitude/Technical Test → In-depth Technical Interview using the STAR model → Cultural Fit).

---

### Application to Work

- **Optimizing Study/Project Workflows**: Applying Docker/Containers and writing basic CI/CD pipelines for academic and real-world projects.
- **Automating Repetitive Tasks**: Utilizing Python/Shell scripts to automate daily manual tasks.
- **Building Dashboards & Data Pipelines**: Applying business/operational metric analytics (GMV, Cost, Fill rate) to data system design.
- **Fostering Teamwork Culture**: Practicing a No-Blame mindset, proactively identifying system root causes during bugs/incidents instead of pointing fingers.
- **Community Contribution**: Actively sharing knowledge (*Share Back*) and participating in AWS Student Builder Group / Cloud Club activities.
