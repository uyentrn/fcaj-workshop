> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
The work environment is dynamic, open, and full of a strong learning spirit. Although much of the program requires a high degree of self-directed study and work, the workspace whenever we come to the office is well-prepared, comfortable, and professional. Everyone in the program is always ready to connect and share, creating a very positive and motivating atmosphere throughout the journey.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The Admin Team and mentors were always closely following our progress, providing timely guidance on both technical questions and career orientation. I am deeply grateful for their dedication—they did not just give direct answers, but also provided space for us to think critically and figure out solutions independently on AWS infrastructure and other complex problems.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The program aligns exceptionally well with my career path in Information Technology / Computer Science. Having direct hands-on experience with AWS Cloud and AI services helped me solidify my foundational knowledge and stay updated with the latest industry-standard technologies.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
This program gave me a dedicated period to focus intensely on diving deep into AWS's rich ecosystem of services. In particular, the sharing sessions on real-world experiences from senior mentors offered valuable insights into practical problem-solving, system architecture design, and career development in Cloud & AI.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
Beside technical knowledge, the greatest takeaway for me after this journey is the quality connections I built. I had the opportunity to meet, learn from, and connect with like-minded peers as well as experienced professionals. The open, supportive culture among members made the FCAJ experience truly fulfilling.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program provided excellent support by granting access to Cloud/AI resources, official learning materials, and detailed hands-on lab guides, all while maintaining time flexibility.

---

### Additional Questions
- What did you find **most satisfying** during your internship?  
It was the focused time that allowed me to sharpen my technical expertise in AWS services, combined with the opportunity to learn from real-world experiences shared by senior mentors and build meaningful professional networks.

- What do you think the company **should improve** for future interns?  
The program could consider organizing more in-person sessions or offering more flexibility for participants to work from the office more frequently. Being physically at the office facilitates direct interactions, speeds up technical troubleshooting, and fosters stronger team bonding.

- If recommending to a friend, would you **suggest they intern here**? Why or why not?  
Definitely **Yes**. This is a fantastic program for anyone looking to dive deep into AWS Cloud & AI, optimize their self-study journey, expand their network with quality connections, and receive valuable guidance from industry experts.

---

### Suggestions & Expectations
- Do you have any suggestions to improve the internship experience?  
I hope the team can increase the frequency or expand office access/hours for participants to work directly in person.

- Would you like to continue this program in the future?  
Yes, I would love to continue accompanying or participating in future programs/projects within the AWS ecosystem.

- Any other comments (free sharing):
Thank you to the FCAJ team and all the mentors for putting so much heart into creating such a meaningful and valuable journey for us!
